Suicide bombs of chikanda


Downtown chikanda
At the brothel of a resthouse
He ferociously sorted them out
One after the other


Wave after wave
Cutting like a razor blade
Now here he moan and goans
Inside ward 2B
frail and Fragile
More bones than steak
Those where lethal sucide bombs.
