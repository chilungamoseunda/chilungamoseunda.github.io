TELL NO ONE

Aunt
Its been months and months
i have endured the attribution
i have carried the cross mutely
but now fail and fragile i have became
No longer can i carry on
On this rutted road to Golgotha
Let me unwrap it all

Aunt
Uncle is a heartless swine
uncle is a brutal beast
yes, my uncle your husband
Throws himself on me
from time to time
And each he does
it he sternly warns me
Tell no one

Analysis

The persona in the poem is a young girl speaking to her aunt about her uncle who has been raping her

Stanza1

The persona laments that has endured the torture for many months now she is voicing out tha she is worry, tied and she can no longer continue to suffer in silence

stanza2

The persona tells her aunt that the uncle is very bad person ie, heartless swine and brutal beast she says that her uncle rapes her from time to time but he warns her not to tell no one
